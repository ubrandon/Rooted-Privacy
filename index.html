Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the MixedRoutes mobile application ("MixedRoutes"), operated by Rooted Social LLC (DBA MixedRoutes). At MixedRoutes, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our social networking and event discovery app.

Who We Are

MixedRoutes is operated by Rooted Social LLC (DBA MixedRoutes), a company dedicated to connecting people through shared experiences and events. Our app helps users discover, create, and participate in various activities and events.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information from you, such as your name, email address, profile information, and other details you provide during registration or while using the App.
  • Event Information: We collect information about events you create, attend, or express interest in, including event details, locations, and preferences.
  • Location Information: If you choose to enable location services, we may collect information about your location to help you discover nearby events and activities.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, including your interactions with other users, events, and the features you access.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including its type, operating system, and other technical details.

How We Use Your Information

  • Event Discovery and Creation: We use your information to help you discover relevant events, create new events, and connect with other users who share similar interests.
  • App Functionality: We use your information to provide and improve the App's features and services, including personalized event recommendations.
  • Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about updates, promotions, event reminders, or other matters related to the App.
  • Analytics: We use usage data to analyze how users interact with the App and to improve its performance and user experience.
  • Security: We use your information to protect the App from unauthorized access and to prevent abuse.

Third-Party Services

  • Analytics Providers: We may use third-party analytics services to help us understand how users interact with our App.
  • Payment Processors: If you make purchases through the App, we may share information with payment processors to complete transactions.
  • Location Services: We may use third-party location services to provide accurate location-based features.
  • Social Media Integration: If you choose to connect your social media accounts, we may share information with those platforms as authorized by you.

Sharing Your Information

  • Third-Party Services: We may share your information with third-party services that help us operate the App, such as analytics providers, payment processors, or location services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users.
  • User-Generated Content: Any content you post on the App, including event details and comments, may be visible to other users.
  • Event Information: Information about events you create or attend may be shared with other users to facilitate event discovery and participation.

Your Rights

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal information through your account settings.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your account and associated data. Please note that some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of certain communications or data collection practices by adjusting your account settings or contacting us.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal data in a portable format.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption of sensitive data, regular security assessments, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where retention is necessary for legal or legitimate business purposes.

Children's Privacy

MixedRoutes is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

If you are using MixedRoutes from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located and our central database is operated. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App following the posting of changes will mean that you accept and agree to the changes.
